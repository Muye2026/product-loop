# Parking Number Plate Demo Brief

## Goal

Create a low-profile temporary parking number plate for car dashboards that can show a phone number clearly when needed and hide it quickly when privacy matters.

## Audience

Primary audience: drivers who park in dense residential, office, or retail environments and want a tidy, heat-resistant dashboard accessory.

## Scenario

The product sits near the windshield on a dashboard. It must remain readable from outside the car, resist cabin heat and sunlight, and avoid looking like a cheap decorative gadget.

## Primary functions

- display a temporary contact number
- hide or reveal the number quickly
- stay stable on a dashboard
- remain readable through windshield glare
- avoid battery or electronics complexity when possible

## Modules and assumptions

- passive number display preferred
- privacy slider or flip-cover mechanism
- silicone anti-slip base or magnetic/weighted base
- optional reflective or photoluminescent digit treatment
- no active LED, e-ink, or rechargeable battery in the preferred baseline

## Hard constraints

- keep the main body close to 125 x 42 x 14 mm
- avoid exposed adhesive on the top visible surface
- tolerate normal parked-car sunlight and cabin heat assumptions
- keep the privacy action one-handed

## Unknowns

- exact digit card supplier and thickness
- silicone pad durometer and heat aging behavior
- whether the user wants premium metal shell or lower-cost plastic shell
